Free Fire Character Olivia Background

  • Name: Olivia.
  • Age: 29.
  • Birthday: October 11.
  • Occupation: Head nurse (8 years).

Olivia is a famous nurse who was known for her ability to bring happiness to her patient. It is said that patients saved by her healing touch, gain an inexplicable strength, and confidence in life.

"Olivia is the best.! She kept me smiling despite my 2 months stay at the hospital. She made me feel comfortable in an uncomfortable situtation"

Olivia met Ford at a high school party and they became lovers since then. Ford lived in the town right next to Olivia. They both worked very hard during high school to get a job and were planning to get married to each other.

However, after 8 years of being a nurse in the hospital, something happened that changed both of their lives forever. One day, a police officer came into the hospital with multiple bullets wounds. This police officer was Andrew after a huge gunfight with a criminal group. According to Andrew, Olivia looks extremely like his fiance, Mary.

Olivia saved the life of Andrew which caused her to be the target of the bad guys. And so both Olivia and Ford got caught in the fight of Andrew and joined the battle royale fight on Bermuda.

Andrew: Am I in heaven?

Olivia: No sir, you are at the hospital - we are going to take good care of you.

Free Fire Character Olivia Skill

Olivia's special survival ability is called Healing Touch. This skill allows her to increase the HP of the player that was revived by her. You can collect Olivia's memory fragments to upgrade her skill up to Level 5 and some other rewards. Here are all the details about Olivia's skill:

  • Healing Touch Level 1: Players who got revived by Olivia will get up with an extra 6 HP.
  • Healing Touch Level 2: Players who got revived by Olivia will get up with an extra 12 HP.
  • Healing Touch Level 3: Players who got revived by Olivia will get up with an extra 18 HP.
  • Healing Touch Level 4: Players who got revived by Olivia will get up with an extra 24 HP.
  • Healing Touch Level 5: Players who got revived by Olivia will get up with an extra 30 HP.

Since Olivia's skill only work when she revive another player, her skill is pretty much useless in Solo matches. Her skill will be more useful in Duo or Squad mode. By giving your teammates more HP when they got revived, your team will be able to get back to the fight faster.

The position of Olivia in a Squad is obviously the Supporter. You can pair Olivia's skill with one of the latest character in Free Fire, Kapella, to further heal your teammates. Antonio is always a viable option for almost any build in Free Fire. Kelly's skill will help you to have more mobility in combats can save your teammates faster.
