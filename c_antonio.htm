Background Story of Antonio In Free Fire

Antonio, the Mad Gangster, was first introduced to Free Fire more than a year ago in December 2018.

"So you think you're gangsta enough to run this place? Antonio will give you a run for your money. The new character, Antonio, did not get his nickname without hustling - he earned it through his mad reputation as the most violent gangster in town." Garena description about Antonio.

  • Age: 30
  • Birthday: August 30.

Antonio  Free Fire was an orphan. Antonio was kidnapped as a kid by a wanted criminal who later brought Antonio to his hideout and raised him there. In Antonio's place, violence is some kind of art that is worshiped by all people here so he had to learn how to live on the street since he was very young.

The criminal has taught Antonio how to fight so Antonio grew up to be a gangster. He constantly fighting with other gangsters on the street for a living. Antonio thinks that if you have nothing in your hands, you must stand up and fight for yourself because no one would care about you.

After a bloody fight between gangs, a mysterious man has come to Antonio and asked why he didn't run although they outnumbered him. Antonio replied by saying that he won't let anyone take what was his. Impressed by his ability, he gave him an offer to fight an evil organization for him in exchange for anything he wants.

Later on, several newspapers reported about a fight between 2 gangsters that caused hundreds of dead and injured people. Antonio was then sentenced to jail for 7 years, 2500 days to be exact, with endless torture and suffering. It turned Antonio into a true monster. He was bought by a mysterious organization out of the prison and was forced to fight on Bermuda since then.

The relationship of other characters with Antonio In Free Fire

In Free Fire, Antonio, Paloma, and Miguel knew about each other when they were kids. Antonio and Miguel were both orphan kids who lived near the riverside while Paloma is the daughter of a rich family. One day, when Antonio, Paloma, and Miguel were playing near the riverside, a wanted criminal appeared and kidnapped three of them.

However, the Special Forces could only recuse Paloma, and Miguel. This is the reason why Miguel wanted to be in the Special Forces when he was a child.

Gangster's Spirit skill of Antonio In Free Fire

Antonio's skill is called Gangster's Spirit, which increases his max HP at the start of the game. You can upgrade his skill up to Level 6, which will give you 35 max HP at the start of the game. Here are the details about Antonio Gangster's Spirit skill.

  • Gangster's Spirit Level 1: Increase your max HP at the start of the match by 10.
  • Gangster's Spirit Level 2: Increase your max HP at the start of the match by 15.
  • Gangster's Spirit Level 3: Increase your max HP at the start of the match by 20.
  • Gangster's Spirit Level 4: Increase your max HP at the start of the match by 25.
  • Gangster's Spirit Level 5: Increase your max HP at the start of the match by 30.
  • Gangster's Spirit Level 6: Increase your max HP at the start of the match by 35.

This is one of the strongest skills in Free Fire. Having a higher max HP allows you to stay in combat longer and win solo fights easily. This skill is just great overall and can be combined with any other skills in Free Fire. Still, it is best to combine this skill with other skills that also increase your resilience in combat such as Andrew's Vest Specialist skill or Jota's Sustained Raid skill.
