Here is the description of Free Fire about Nikita. Nikita is a professional bodyguard. Her most important assignment is to protect and escort Caroline, the daughter of the chairman of the Free Fire's organization. Growing up, Nikita was an air rifle marksman in school, which helped hone her agility and keen senses. To achieve her objectives, she infiltrated the organization and become Carolyn's bodyguard.

Nikita actually isn't the real name of Free Fire Nikita character. Nikita is her code name. Her real name is Yuna.

While she appears to be the bodyguard of Caroline, the daughter of the chairman of the Free Fire's organization, she is actually a spy who penetrated the organization to steal information. Nikita worked with Moco to leak the information she got from the organization.

The reason why she did this was that the organization kidnapped her friend called Min Ho. When searching for information on Min Ho's computer, Nikita knew that he was investigating this organization call Cobra.

While she is working at the organization, Nikita saw someone who looks just like her friend Min Ho but he didn't recognize her and said he worked for the organization. However, Nikita didn't believe it and stayed there to confirm the truth.

Free Fire Nikita Character Skill

Misha's special survival ability is called Firearms Expert, which allows her to reload her Submachine gun faster. You can collect Misha's memory fragments to upgrade her skill up to Level 6 and some other rewards. Here all the details about her skill:

  • Firearms Expert Level 1: She can decrease Submachine gun reload time by 4%.
  • Firearms Expert Level 2: She can decrease Submachine gun reload time by 8%.
  • Firearms Expert Level 3: She can decrease Submachine gun reload time by 12%.
  • Firearms Expert Level 4: She can decrease Submachine gun reload time by 16%.
  • Firearms Expert Level 5: She can decrease Submachine gun reload time by 20%.
  • Firearms Expert Level 6: She can decrease Submachine gun reload time by 24%.

The skill of Free Fire Nikita character is quite useful but it is not as good as other skills such as Antonio's skill or Clu's skill. With a fast reload time, Nikita character in Free Fire can re-engage faster than enemies and get the upper hand in long heated battles. You would want to have a more aggressive playstyle with this skill so it would be best to pair it with skills that make her more flexible in battles.

Jota's skill you help you regain HP after a fight using SMG, which synergy well with Nikita. Clu's skill will allow you to locate the locations of enemies and rush to the right place. Miguel's skill will give you EP after each kill to heal more after each fight.
