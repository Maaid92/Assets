Story Of Misha in Free Fire

  • Gender: Female
  • Age: 19
  • Birthday: Jul 26th

According to the brief introduction of Free Fire, Misha is an extremely talented racer. Her bold attitude and crazy driving skills earned her the Racequeen title from the racing community. She always tackles challenges head-on, she became more and more popular as she joined more tournaments. To Misha, there are no curve roads, the road forward is always straight.

Character Misha in Free Fire was based on the character Cyndi in Final Fantasy 15.

Misha and Maxim are sister and brother. Misha's hobby is racing and live streaming. She hates his brother's gluttony because Maxim always ate up everything in the fridge. Misha always knew that Maxim was in love with Kelly but he was too shy to confess it. Misha encouraged his brother to do it but then Kelly was kidnapped before he was able to do it.

Misha character in Free Fire is bolder than his brother and she usually participates in underground racing tournaments for money. Misha had a racing buddy called Eddie, who she admired for his racing skill. One day, a black car tried to crash into Misha to kill her but Eddie has sacrificed himself to save Misha.

In order to find the one who killed her friend and find justice, Misha joined a race where she thought that the black car would appear. Misha won this race and she was given the Racequeen title but she didn't find the guy with the black car. Later on, she was kidnapped along with Kelly to Purgatory.

The one who kidnapped Misha and Kelly was Paloma. When Maxim tried to recuse them on the plane, the plane got broken and then all of them ended up in Purgatory.

Ability Of Misha in Free Fire

Misha's special survival ability is called Afterburner, which allows her to drive faster and take less damage while in a vehicle. You can collect Misha's memory fragments to upgrade her skill up to Level 6 and some other rewards. Here all the details about her skill:

  •  Afterburner Level 1: Driving speed increased by 2%, damage taken while in a vehicle decreased by 5%.
  •  Afterburner Level 2: Driving speed increased by 4%, damage taken while in a vehicle decreased by 10%.
  •  Afterburner Level 3: Driving speed increased by 6%, damage taken while in a vehicle decreased by 15%.
  •  Afterburner Level 4: Driving speed increased by 8%, damage taken while in a vehicle decreased by 20%.
  •  Afterburner Level 5: Driving speed increased by 10%, damage taken while in a vehicle decreased by 25%.
  •  Afterburner Level 6: Driving speed increased by 12%, damage taken while in a vehicle decreased by 30%.

While the skill of Misha character in Free Fire is not the best skill in Free Fire, it has its own perks, especially when you want to have fun and crush every single player in the game in your car. With the increased speed and the reduced damage, it will be harder for enemies to dodge you or take you down when you are trying to run them over.

With this playstyle, you can combine her skills with Antonio's skill to increase your Max HP at the start of the game and the skill of the upcoming character "Luqueta" to increase your Max HP after each kill. This will turn you into a real unstoppable tank to run over other players.

Other than that, her skill is not very helpful in normal situations as Free Fire maps are generally small so cars are not really needed.
